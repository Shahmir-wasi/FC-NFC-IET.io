About FC NFC IET

FC NFC IET is a student-run football society dedicated to promoting teamwork and sportsmanship. We organize regular matches within the university and compete against other teams, providing a platform for players to showcase their skills and passion for the game. Join us in celebrating the spirit of football!

Acknowledgments

We thank our amazing members and players for their passion and dedication, and our organizing team for their hard work. Together, we make FC NFC IET thrive!
